A PINCH ABOUT ME

I have always been passionate about photography, but my first real approach was when on the street I found a camera with 3 lenses and from there I change my life, before then I used my grandfather's camera which had a solar panel as an exposure meter. ... stuff of other times. After I finished classical studies at the high school I took a course in photography and a course of DoP at the cinecitta's in Rome, after completing my studies I started working for Sky News in Italy as an assistant, first then a cameraman for six years. With the Italian crisis I moved to great britain and I am currently a student at De montfort University, but I never stop taking pictures.
